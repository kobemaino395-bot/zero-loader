Encrypt shellcode

Runs Encrypt.py. Compresses and encrypts the payload, producing data.enc and a decrypt key. Select an Arweave wallet to embed its address in Payload.h now — then hit ⚡ Upload in the history row to publish to Arweave without re-selecting a wallet.

New key, nonce, and XOR rotation on every run.
